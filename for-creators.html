Creator FAQ

Is it free to join?

Applying is free. We don't charge creators to be represented. We earn when we win you a partnership you actually wanted.

Why is there a waitlist?

We cap how many creators we take on each month so the roster stays genuinely values-aligned. Interest has been overwhelming, so responses can take a little while. We read every application by hand.

How do I get in faster?

We prioritise creators referred by someone already on the roster. Add their name on the application and you move up the queue.

Do I need a huge following?

No. We represent everyone from micro creators to household names. Belief scales where budget doesn't — engagement and genuine affinity matter more than raw reach.

What is the Passion Score?

A composite of profile depth, audience, engagement and the strength of your brand affinity. It decides where you rank on the board. Try the two-minute quiz for an indicative number.

What does "top six" mean?

The six brands you'd genuinely represent for free, ranked in order. We pursue them proactively, from the top down. We never ask you to perform love you don't feel.
