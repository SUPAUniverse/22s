This policy explains how twentytwo ("we", "us") collects and uses your personal data when you use this website, in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

When you apply to the waitlist or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the details you give us: your name, email, social handle, category, audience size, the brands in your top six, any referral, and what you tell us about your work. We also collect basic, anonymous analytics about how the site is used.

Why we use it

To review your application and consider you for representation, to contact you about partnerships, to send you our newsletter if you opted in, and to improve the site. Our lawful bases are your consent and our legitimate interest in running the agency.

Who we share it with

We use trusted processors to run the site and store data (for example our hosting and database providers). We do not sell your data. We only share it where necessary to provide our services or where the law requires.

How long we keep it

We keep application and account data for as long as you are on the roster or under consideration, and for a reasonable period after, then delete it. Newsletter data is kept until you unsubscribe.

Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or delete your data, withdraw consent, or object to processing at any time. Email us at Passion@supa.social. You can also complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk).

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies for essential function and anonymous analytics. You can choose your preferences in the cookie banner. See more in our cookie notice.

Contact

Questions about this policy: Passion@supa.social.
