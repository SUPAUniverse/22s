About

Built by a maker.

twentytwo was founded by Matthew Peltier, who built and ran an in-house film and audio team that delivered 300+ campaigns for brands from Lidl to LinkedIn, Vodafone to AXA.

That's the difference. We don't just broker deals, we come from production. We understand what makes content true on camera, because we've spent a decade making it. We've built the whole platform around the creator who means it.

North London roots, a deep love of music and culture, and a simple conviction: the work is better when the passion is real. Passion first. Always.
